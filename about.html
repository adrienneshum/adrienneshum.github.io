    hey there, friend!
    my name is Chaereen (shur-EEN).

    I’m a content creator, pistachio gelato and mango sticky rice enthusiast, and Los Angeles native now based in Philadelphia. I care deeply for people and their well-being, and I’m passionate about mindfully elevating the stories of sincere individuals, groups, cultures, and environments.

    I graduated from Penn with a B.A. in Psychology and Fine Arts and honed in on the disciplines of positive psychology and digital media. I also extensively studied languages and spent a semester chasing dazzling city lights with my camera in Tokyo.

    Currently, you’ll find me slinging cappuccinos and sketching chalkboard art at Function Coffee Labs, biking along the Schuylkill at sunset with my obnoxiously pink helmet, and experimenting with color wheels once a week for @thejadebook.

    Thanks for visiting my little home on the internet! Hope you enjoy your stay.
